The Titanomachy

Zeus led the Olympians in a decade-long war against the Titans, his father Cronus and the elder gods. With cunning and might, he freed his siblings from Cronus's belly and claimed dominion over the cosmos, dividing realms among his brothers.

"From chaos rose order, and order demanded a king."

Birth of Wisdom

Athena emerged fully armored from Zeus's forehead, born of thought itself. Goddess of wisdom, warfare, and crafts, she became Athens's patron and champion of heroes. Her owl sees through darkness, her shield turns enemies to stone.

"In wisdom lies the true strength of mortals and gods alike."

Lord of the Seas

God of Light & Music

Apollo brings light, truth, and artistic inspiration. Twin of Artemis, he rides the sun chariot across the sky, his lyre creating melodies that move mountains. Prophet of Delphi, his oracle speaks truths mortals fear to hear.

"Truth illuminates, even when it burns."
